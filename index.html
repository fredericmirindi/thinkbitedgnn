Our Mission

ThinkBit Edge Corp is dedicated to empowering youth, especially those from underrepresented groups, by providing equitable access to high-quality education in new technologies, including artificial intelligence (AI). Our purpose is to inspire, educate, and support the next generation of innovators by making learning opportunities in emerging tech accessible, engaging, and inclusive.

About ThinkBit Edge

Our story, vision, and values driving inclusive technology education

Founder Story

ThinkBit Edge Corp was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to ensure that every young person, regardless of their background, has access to the tools and knowledge needed to thrive in our increasingly digital world. Our founders recognized that traditional barriers to technology education were leaving too many talented minds behind, particularly those from underrepresented communities.

Our Core Values

Equity

Ensuring fair access to technology education for all youth, regardless of background or circumstances.

Empowerment

Building confidence and skills that enable youth to become leaders in their communities.

Innovation

Embracing cutting-edge approaches to make learning engaging and relevant.

Community

Fostering connections and collaboration that strengthen our collective impact.
