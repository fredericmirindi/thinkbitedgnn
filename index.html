Our Story

Founded by passionate educators and technologists, ThinkBit Edge Corp. began as a community project to close the opportunity gap in tech education. Today, we partner with schools, community centers, and industry leaders to deliver transformative learning experiences.

Our Approach

We practice community-centered, culturally responsive education, ensuring that each learner sees themselves reflected in the curriculum and future of technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join ThinkBit Edge programs?

Youth aged 8-24 from all backgrounds are welcome! Scholarships are available for underrepresented students.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is necessary. Our programs are designed for beginners through advanced learners.
